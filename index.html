ABOUT

Our family has been in the restaurant business for a very long time. Nowadays we can proudly boast our reputation for a well known Italian restaurant in our area. We are famous for the fabulous authentic cuisine, professional chef and dedicated staff. Choose any dish from the menu which can help you to experience the real taste of Italy.

OUR PASSION

We want to share our passion for the excellent food and great service. We cook our dishes from the freshest ingredients. We specialize in homemade classic Italian pastas, sauces, soups, desserts and much more in a warm friendly atmosphere.

HISTORY

Our Restaurant was established in May, 25 1987. Since then it has grown to more than 30 restaurants worldwide and plans continue to extend, that is not the end of the story...We have a passion for the Italian way of life and want too share it with people, no matter where they're from, just good food for everyone.

BEST RECIPES

A lot of people all around the world admire Italian food. Big lovers of Italian culinary majesty, pizza, lasagna, different kinds of pasta, meatballs and spaghetti, specialty sauces, tasty desserts. Our menu provides you with the mix of fresh seasonal ingredients, refined goods and the best recipes and techniques. We do our best to serve you delicious food.

MENU

In collaboration with working background the kitchen creates modern, seasonal and focused on your pleasure dishes. We provide you with daily self-made bread, sourdough pizza, roasted fish-meat-vegetables and many more.
